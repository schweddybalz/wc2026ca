Group Stage — 64 pts available
For each of the 12 groups, pick which team finishes 1st, 2nd, and optionally 3rd.
You must pick exactly 8 third-place teams to advance (4 groups will have no 3rd pick).

+1 pt bonus if you also got their exact finishing position (1st / 2nd / 3rd)

Maximum 64 points in the group stage (32 teams × 2 pts each)
